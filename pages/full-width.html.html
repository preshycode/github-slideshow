<h1>LETS TALK <h6> - ABOUT CAMPUS AWARDS

The CAMPUS AWARDS is a production of CAMPUS VIBES EVENTS, established in 2018 to recognize students striving to attain success and Greatness in line with their academic pursuit and also the special recognition Awards to personalities and great supporter's who has constantly stood by and invested in ensuring of the Growth and success of the young people of our nation

The Awards  are grouped into  four (4) categories  which  are; Entertainment, Enterprise, Leadership Development and Award of Special Recognition to individuals and brands who had been supporting Academic, Entertainment and Entrepreneurship over the years.  These categories are well carved out to cover every area of human endeavor thus leaving room to reward excellence from any angle. Over the years, event planners, have really not seen the need to reward thriving youths doing exploit in what they fimknd joy doing, rather the entire focus most of the time, is on pageantry which of course is not bad. However, Campus Award came into existence to fill that vacuum hence, leaving room for every enterprise to be rewarded. We at Campus Vibes Events, understands the role  of reward system in spurring youths towards daring for more..

It is an Award Ceremony with  unique categories, that have  been set aside only for Nigerian students, within the university, polytechnic and colleges of education. It serves the purpose of promoting excellence / appreciating Creativity and fostering entrepreneural attitude among Nigerian students in campuses all over the nation. The third edition of Campus Awards (CAMPUS AWARDS SOUTH SOUTH) with the unprecedented theme, the HERO'S EDITION is featuring all campus in the South South geopolitical zone of the Country, cutting across DELTA STATE, AKWA IBOM STATE, CROSS RIVER STATE, BAYELSA STATE, RIVER STATE AND EDO STATE
